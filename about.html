About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Marcel Regeci, and I'm a software engineer and medior Java developer. I was born on November 5th, 1996, and I'm passionate about technology and sports.

I graduated with an Engineering degree in computer science from the Technical University in Kosice two years ago, and since then, I've been working as a software engineer. My expertise lies in Java development, and I'm always looking for new and exciting challenges to take on.

In addition to my work, I also enjoy spending time with my family and friends. I believe that a happy life is all about balance, and for me, that means making time for the people who matter most.

When I'm not working or socializing, you can usually find me pursuing one of my hobbies. I'm an avid sports enthusiast, and I love playing and watching a variety of sports. I'm also an indoor climber, and I love the challenge and physicality of this sport.

Another hobby of mine is flying my DJi drone. I find it incredibly rewarding to capture aerial footage and explore the world from a different perspective.
